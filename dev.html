Accessing Devmode

In your Chrome home page, reach the extensions page by clicking on the extensions icon.

Turn on Devmode, then click "Load Unpacked" to select your extension folder.

Now with the extension installed, go ahead and try to access Youtube. As you can see, all of it's contents are entirely gone!

If that is the case, congratulation, you have made your own private extension to help improve your productivity,
