Publishing Your Extension

First, you will go to the Chrome dev site and select the "Developer Dashboard".

There will be a $5 fee before being able to publish your extension.

Then select the "New Item" button and you will be prompted to upload the .zip file.

After uploading, there will be some forms to fill out before publishing.
